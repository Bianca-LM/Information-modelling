About the project

Cool down! is a magazine built by Bianca La Manna as a project for the course "Information Modelling & Web Technologies", of the Prof. Fabio Vitali. It is one of the courses of the Master's Degree "Digital Humanities & Digital Knowledge" of the University of Bologna.
